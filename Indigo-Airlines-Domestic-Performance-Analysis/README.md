## IndiGo Domestic Airline Performance Analysis

Analyzed IndiGo flight operations, revenue, costs, profitability, route performance, and aircraft utilization to identify operational trends and business insights.

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing IndiGo Airlines flight operations and financial performance using Excel, Power BI, and business intelligence techniques.

The dashboard provides insights into ticket revenue, cargo revenue, operating costs, profitability, route-wise performance, aircraft efficiency, load factors, customer satisfaction, and monthly performance trends.

## Insights

* IndiGo domestic airline generated a total yearly revenue of ₹438.3 million.

* The month-on-month revenue growth rate is 7.2%.

* July recorded the highest number of passengers during the year.

* Bengaluru–Delhi is the busiest route with a total passenger count of 8,045.

* Most routes show good customer satisfaction levels, with the Madras–Delhi route having the 
  highest satisfaction rate, achieving 36 satisfied flights out of 58 flights. 

* Despite generating strong revenue from ticket sales and cargo operations,
  high fuel and operating costs are reducing overall profit.

* Flight delays have a direct impact on operational performance and customer experience.

## Conclusion

The analysis provides a broad view on IndiGo's operational and financial performance. 
While the airline generates good amount of revenue, high fuel and operating costs significantly impact profitability.
Optimizing route efficiency, aircraft utilization, and operational expenses can improve overall business performance and support sustainable growth.
